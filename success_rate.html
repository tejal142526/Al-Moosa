How many IVF cycles should I try?

The number of recommended cycles varies, but many couples try 1-3 IVF cycles before achieving success. Our specialists will guide you based on your situation.

Is egg freezing safe?

Yes, egg freezing is a safe and well-established procedure with minimal risks.

Who should consider PGT?

PGT is recommended for couples with a history of genetic disorders, recurrent miscarriages, or failed IVF cycles, as well as women of advanced maternal age.
